Shear Force & Bending Moment Diagram Plot

We have made a dynamic value plotter to show the plots of Shear Force and Bending Moment for 3 different cases---
1. A point load is given between two different supports.
2. A uniform load is given along the length of the beam.
3. A linearly varying load is given along with the length of the beam. It calculated for both increasing and decreasing loads
Note: All the 3 cases have been optimally suited for all possible cases, given the load is in between support.
We worked under the guidance of Dr. Hirshikesh, Assistant Professor at the Department of Mechanical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur.
